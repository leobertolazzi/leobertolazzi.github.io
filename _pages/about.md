Ciao, I'm Leonardo!

I'm a master's student in Cognitive Science at [CIMeC](https://www.cimec.unitn.it/en), University of Trento, Italy. My research interests lie at the intersection of Cognitive Science and AI, with a focus on evaluation methods for AI systems, multimodal learning, and conversational AI.

I'm currently working on my master's thesis, which is titled "Information Seeking Strategies in Large Language Models and Humans: Insights from the 20-Question Game". In this project, I'm using a variety of evaluation methods to compare the performance of large language models and humans on the 20 Questions game.

If you have any questions about my research or if you're interested in collaborating, please feel free to get in touch!
